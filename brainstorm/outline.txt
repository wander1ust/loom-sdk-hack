PERSONALIZED GREETING MAKER 

	Send & Receive personalized video messages + optional e-gifts
		Send long, sentimental messages or keep msgs short & sweet
		
		What's better than trying to read a paper greeting card in someone else's voice,  picturing their faces in your mind? 
		Watching & listening to a video playback/recording of them speaking/narrating directly to you.

	Eternal playback that lasts 'forever'
		Capture, save & collect special virtual moments & gift receipts that last a lifetime

		Miss You, Thinking of You		
		...and a bunch more

SENDER

	1. Record video greeting
		- select template theme (premade / custom)
		- record voiceover · choose GIF + tune (optional)

	2. Create, attach & e-wrap gift	
		- gift options
			digital - gift card, ticket, subscription, Groupon, NFT
			physical - package delivery	
		gift == option ? render matching img :
			default to generic mystery item or user uploaded image
		- choose wrapping paper
		- generate custom e-gift link 			

	3. Add text message
		- embed Loom video	
		- append unique gift url (to body message + as video action item CTA)

	4. Review & Send	
		- set delivery date + time
			- reschedule/cancel as needed before greeting is sent (in case of incorrect user input, postponed dates, delayed est. package arrivals, etc.)
		- send via email, DM on Slack, Discord, LinkedIn, social media
